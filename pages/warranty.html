Warranty & Support Policy

Clear coverage terms so you know exactly what's protected — on new purchases, refurbished devices, and every repair we carry out.

Coverage at a glance

How long your purchase or repair is protected

All warranty periods below start from the date of purchase or repair collection, shown on your receipt or ticket.

New Computers

12 months manufacturer/dealer warranty on hardware faults not caused by misuse.

Refurbished (Grade A)

3 months warranty covering tested components — battery health is disclosed at sale.

Repairs & Parts

30–90 days on the specific part and labor, depending on the repair type — confirmed on your job card.

Accessories

30 days replacement warranty on UPS units, cables, chargers and peripherals for manufacturing defects.

Service commitment

Our turnaround SLA

Standard repair

Most screen, battery and keyboard repairs: 48 hours average from diagnosis approval to pickup.

Express repair

Same-day priority service available for an added fee when parts are in stock — ask at drop-off.

Complex / parts-on-order

Motherboard-level faults or imported parts: 5–10 working days, with status updates via WhatsApp.

How to claim

Making a warranty claim is simple

  1. Bring the device and your original receipt or ticket number to our Luwum Street shop, or message us on WhatsApp first.

  2. Our technician re-diagnoses the fault free of charge to confirm it's covered.

  3. If covered, repair or replacement proceeds at no extra cost. If it's outside warranty, we'll quote you before any work.

What's not covered

Physical/liquid damage after purchase, unauthorized repairs by a third party, software issues caused by user-installed programs, and normal wear like battery capacity loss outside the disclosed period.

Have a warranty question?

Send us your receipt or ticket number on WhatsApp and we'll confirm your coverage in minutes.
