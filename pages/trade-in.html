Trade-In & Upgrade Program

Trade in your working laptop or desktop and put its value toward a new or refurbished machine.

How it works

Upgrade without paying full price twice

1. Estimate
Use the calculator below for a rough value, or bring it in directly.

2. Inspection
Our technician checks the device in-store — usually 15–20 minutes.

3. Final offer
We confirm the trade-in credit based on condition, age and working parts.

4. Upgrade
Apply the credit toward any new or refurbished machine in the shop.
