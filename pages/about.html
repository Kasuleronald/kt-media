About KT-Media Tech

A Kampala-based team that sells computers and accessories, keeps them running long after the sale, and builds the apps and AI skills businesses need next.

Our story

Started by people who fix things, not just sell them

KT-Media Tech was founded in Kampala by technicians who got tired of seeing customers sold computers with no support afterward. We built the business the other way around: we are repair people first, and that's why we know what to sell, and to whom.

Today we operate from Kampala town, Luwum Street, Master Building Shop L-21 — selling new and quality pre-owned computers and accessories, and running a full repair and maintenance workshop for individuals, schools, NGOs and small businesses across the city.
